About SwasthCare

SwasthCare is a comprehensive Hospital Management System designed to streamline hospital operations, improve patient care, and optimize resource utilization. Our system integrates key functionalities such as OPD queue management, bed availability tracking, and patient admission management into a single, easy-to-use platform.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a robust and scalable solution that enhances the efficiency of hospital operations while ensuring a better experience for patients and staff. We aim to bridge the gap between traditional hospital management practices and modern technological advancements.

Key Features

  • Real-time OPD Queue Management
  • Accurate Bed Availability Tracking
  • Streamlined Patient Admission Process
  • User-friendly Dashboards for Staff and Patients
